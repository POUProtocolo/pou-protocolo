## Official Differentiators

1. **Treasury Protection as a Core Principle**  
   The protocol’s financial base is treated as a central element of operational consistency.

2. **Controlled Issuance with Reserve-Based Mitigation**  
   Its structure is designed to reduce unnecessary issuance pressure through reserve-oriented logic.

3. **Timed Claim Logic**  
   The protocol includes a structured claim timeline as part of its participation mechanics.

4. **USDT-Structured Participation Mechanics**  
   USDT is used as the operational participation reference.

5. **Protocol-Oriented Structure, Not Just a Token**  
   POU PROTOCOLO V24 is positioned as a protocol with internal rules and functional coherence.

6. **Compliance-Ready Architecture**  
   The structure was designed to support future compliance and wallet screening integrations, subject to operational implementation.

7. **Internal Economic Coherence**  
   The project prioritizes discipline between participation, issuance, reserve management, and circulation.

8. **Progressive Public Transparency**  
   The project is designed to evolve with verifiable public presence and gradual ecosystem credibility.
